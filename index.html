Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How long does company registration take?

Company registration with CIPC typically takes 3-5 working days. We handle the entire process — name reservation, registration documents, and final certificate delivery.

What documents do I need for tax clearance?

You'll need your company registration certificate, proof of address, ID copy, and recent financial statements. We guide you through every document and submit on your behalf to SARS.

Do you help with funding applications?

Yes! We assist with business plan preparation, financial projections, and identifying suitable funding opportunities — from government grants to private investors.

Can you help if my business is already registered?

Absolutely. We offer marketing, coaching, compliance updates, website building, and access to growth opportunities for existing businesses at any stage.

What are your consultation fees?

Your first consultation is completely free. After understanding your needs, we provide a transparent quote with no hidden costs. Service packages are tailored to your budget.
